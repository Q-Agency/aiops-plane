Cold open - say this before you touch the app

“Everyone on your team is already coding with AI - different tools, different models, pasting whatever into whatever. You can’t see it, can’t enforce it, can’t prove a standard was met, can’t keep code in the building. A copilot inside someone’s IDE is ungovernable by design. The only way to control it is to take implementation headless - into a harness that runs only approved tools and models, with a human accountable at every gate. Let me show you. Live. Three minutes.”
